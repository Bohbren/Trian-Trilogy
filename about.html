Meet the Author

Scott is a native of Iowa and a retired disabled veteran. Scott has always been a student of the nature of people and the world around him. After earning his bachelor's degree in behavioral sciences and a master's in organizational performance from Bellevue University, Scott has devoted himself to helping others as a certified substance use counselor. He is working on a second master's in Clinical Mental Health Therapy. His other passion has always been writing. Fantasy and Adventure is his genre of choice.

Always fascinated by history, mythology, and theology, Scott has combined his passions to create his first literary creation, The Trian Trilogy.
